Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the operator of intimewig.com uses data in connection with the Meta app named fullc11. The app is used for marketing operations, including creating ads, managing advertising accounts, and analyzing advertising performance.

Who We Are

The operator of intimewig.com maintains this website and the Meta developer app fullc11. For privacy questions, contact us at info@intimewig.com.

What Data We Process

Depending on the permissions approved by Meta and the assets you connect, the app may process:

  • Meta Business, ad account, Page, campaign, ad set, ad, creative, and audience metadata.
  • Advertising performance metrics such as spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, and return metrics.
  • Account identifiers, asset identifiers, permission status, and access token status needed to operate the app.
  • Contact information you voluntarily provide when requesting support or access.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information through this app, and we do not request Meta permissions unrelated to marketing operations.

How We Use Data

  • To create, update, review, and manage advertising campaigns.
  • To analyze advertising performance and produce marketing reports.
  • To troubleshoot account, permission, delivery, and reporting issues.
  • To secure the app, prevent misuse, and keep operational audit records.

Sharing and Restrictions

We do not sell Meta Platform Data. We do not use Meta Platform Data for surveillance, credit eligibility, employment eligibility, housing eligibility, or any purpose unrelated to advertising and marketing operations. We may share limited data with service providers who help us host, secure, analyze, or operate the app, only as needed and under confidentiality or data protection obligations.

Storage and Retention

We retain operational data only for as long as needed to provide the app, maintain advertising records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and improve marketing operations. Access tokens and connected asset permissions are revoked or deleted when access is no longer needed or when you request deletion. Aggregated or de-identified reporting data may be retained when it no longer identifies a person or connected account.

Data Deletion Instructions

To request deletion of data connected to the fullc11 app, email info@intimewig.com with the subject Data Deletion Request - fullc11. Include the Meta Business ID, ad account ID, Page ID, or email address associated with the request so we can locate the relevant records.

After verifying the request, we will delete or de-identify eligible data and revoke app access where applicable. We aim to respond within 30 days unless a shorter period is required by applicable law.

A standalone copy of these instructions is available at intimewig.com/data-deletion.html.

Your Choices

  • You can remove the app's access from your Meta Business or account settings.
  • You can contact us to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of eligible data.
  • You can stop using the app at any time.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect app credentials, connected account data, and reports. No online service is perfectly secure, but we limit access to people and systems that need the data for authorized work.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when our app, data practices, or legal obligations change. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
